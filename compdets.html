Rules & Guidelines

The team behind the inaugural KLSMC will do their absolutely best to ensure the integrity of the competition, however a portion of this is also dependent on the participants and their conduct. As such we want to emphasise the following rules as a means to maximise everyone's enjoyment of the competition:

  • Use of the internet; online calculators / AI chatbots such as Desmos, WolframAlpha or ChatGPT are discouraged, as all teams are required to make the small intuitive leaps to answer difficult questions, on their own.

  • Misconduct with other teams is prohibited. This includes answer sharing between same school teams, and general bad manners towards any team.
