Hello! We are Alice Smith CS & Maths Prefects

We are the Alice Smith Maths & Computer Science prefect team of 2024-25. Our mission is to inspire more international school students, of all ages, across Kuala Lumpur to engage in problem solving through hosting Maths / Computer Science competitions.

About the KL Coding Cup

Unlike every other coding cup hosted, KL Coding Cup 2025 @ KLASS will be a mixture of both physical and classical algorithmic thinking, we a fusion of quick paced problem solving and detailed, implemented scenarios where algorithmic problem solving is crucial. Beware! Not every question is trial and error as some may lead to a deduction of points and may even lead to a negative score!
