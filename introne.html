Round 1 : Find the Victim.

Question Overview

First up, in the evening, you have been tasked with identifying the victim.
An innocent passerby captured an individual with blood stains rushing hastily towards the parking staircase with blood dripping onto the carpet floor. A local biologist takes samples of the blood stains and returns to his lab attempting to unravel the blood type of the suspect along with the victim. He concludes that 2 of his samples suggest that the Blood Type is undiscovered and is represented in integer form. Additionally, when inquired by security, the innocent passerby states in a hurrying manner ‘ In the corner of my eye, I caught a glimpse of a person. They had bright blue eyes and long blonde hair and I caught sight of him at the banquet hall initially before he dramatically collapsed’. Enter the blood type to proceed in identifying the name of the victim

Output:
i) For each test case, your mission guide has told you to output a single line containing Case (A): B1, B2 , B3, ….. BQ, whereby A is the test case number that begins from 1, and yi is the answer to the i-th query in the input.

ii) To determine the blood type code, calculate the sum of all digits in Case Test 6.

Hint: A substring is a contiguous sequence of characters that appears within another string, maintaining the same order of characters.

However, due to the complex nature of the mission, your mission guide has informed you that there are numerous limits to be followed.

Limits:
– T is greater than or equal to 1 but less than or equal to 100
– Pi is greater than or equal to 1 but less than or equal to the length of X
– Si is greater than or equal to 1 but less than or equal to the length of Y
